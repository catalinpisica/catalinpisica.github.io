About Me

I come from a small village in Moldova, where my journey into the world of data began. From a young age, I embarked on a journey of self-reliance, working, studying, and living independently since I was 17. I consider myself a dedicated and results-driven individual who has always been passionate about turning vision into reality. My journey into the field of data analytics and marketing began with my Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Babes-Bolyai University. Building upon this foundation, I pursued a Master's degree in Marketing Analytics and Data Science at the University of Groningen. This program deepened my understanding of advanced data science and analytics.

My academic experience provided me with a strong analytical mindset and equipped me with the skills to excel in diverse work environments. I'm skilled in tools like R, Python, SQL, PowerBI, and Excel and love to analyze, visualize, and derive insights from data. My work revolves around making data useful and turning it into a valuable asset for businesses and organizations.
